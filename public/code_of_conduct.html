Songfarm Order of Conduct

The following items establish the Songfarm Order of Conduct.
Each item serves to outline the conduct by which participants of this and any forthcoming Songcirlce are asked to abide by.
Please observe the following:

Songfarm Order of Conduct

  1. Respect the craft
  2. Come prepared
  3. Listen to eachother's songs
  4. Offer positive, constructive feedback
  5. Allow everyone the chance to be heard
  6. Focus on the song, not the writer
  7. Enjoy the process
